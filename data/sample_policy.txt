The Pradhan Mantri Awas Yojana - Gramin (PMAY-G) is a flagship scheme of the Ministry of Rural Development. 
It aims to provide 'Housing for All' by the year 2024. 
In the state of Maharashtra, the Pune district has shown significant progress in the implementation of PMAY-G. 
The scheme targets rural families living in kutcha houses or who are houseless. 
So far, over 1.2 lakh houses have been sanctioned in Pune, with a fund utilization rate of 75%.
The Ministry of Rural Development administers the fund, while the local Zilla Parishad handles the ground implementation.
Beneficiaries are selected based on the Socio-Economic and Caste Census (SECC) 2011 data.
